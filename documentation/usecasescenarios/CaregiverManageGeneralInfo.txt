Use Case Scenario :: Caregiver Manage General Info

Primary Flow:
1) Caregiver logs in
2) Caregiver select patient from patient list view
3) Caregiver navigates from taps to general info
4) Caregiver selects "edit" button
5) Caregiver edits general info
6) Caregiver confirms edit to general info
7) Caregiver general information is updated

Alternate Flow:
6b) Caregiver declines edit to general info
7b) Caregiver general information is not updated
