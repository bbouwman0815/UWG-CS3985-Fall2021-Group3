Use Case Scenario :: Medical Personnel Manage patient caregiver

Primary Flow:
1) Medical Personnel logs in
2) Medical Personnel select patient from patient list view
3) Medical Personnel navigates from tap bar to general info
4) Medical Personnel selects "Add Caregiver" or "Remove Caregiver" button
5) Medical Personnel general information is updated

Alternate Flow:
4b) Medical Personnel declines edit to add caregiver
5b) Patient caregiver is not updated
